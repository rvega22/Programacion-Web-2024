Siamese Cat

Temperament: Vocal, Social, Intelligent, Curious, Active, Affectionate

Lifespan: 15 - 20 years

Description: Siamese cats are known for their elegant and distinctive appearance, with a short, light-colored coat that contrasts with dark markings on their face, ears, paws, and tail. They are highly social and vocal cats, often "talking" to their owners. Siamese cats are loyal, affectionate, and love being around people, though they can be quite demanding of attention.

Kid-friendly: 5

Dog-friendly: 4

Origin: Thailand
